Hiking & Trail Running Adventures

The line between hiking & trail running has always been blurry for me. I have a hard time classifying my adventures into hikes and trail runs. I have had flat hikes averaging over 3 mph and steep runs averaging less than 3 mph. Usually, if my main objective is to go fast and light, I call it a run and everything else is a hike in my book. I have been hiking regularly more or less since 2011. But recently I started dabbling in trail running in 2020. Here are reports from some of my longer trail runs and some fun hiking adventures.

  • Taconia Crest Trail and Devil's Path

    62 miles hiking, 32 miles biking in two days (Sep 25-26, 2020)

  • Mahoosuc Traverse

  • White Mountains Hut Traverse
